🐱 Cat Breeds

Meet our feline friends — from elegant Persians to majestic Maine Coons.

Persian

Gentle, calm, and sweet-natured. The Persian's luxurious coat and pansy-like face make them the glamour puss of cats.

Calm & Gentle 12-17 yrs

Maine Coon

The gentle giant. Sociable, intelligent, and playful, Maine Coons are one of the largest domesticated cat breeds.

Sociable & Playful 12-15 yrs

Siamese

Vocal, affectionate, and striking. Siamese cats are known for their blue almond-shaped eyes and social personality.

Vocal & Affectionate 15-20 yrs

Bengal

Wildly beautiful and endlessly energetic. Bengals have a distinctive spotted coat and love water and climbing.

Energetic & Curious 12-16 yrs

Ragdoll

Docile, gentle, and affectionate. Ragdolls go limp when picked up, earning their unique name.

Docile & Gentle 12-15 yrs

British Shorthair

Easy-going, loyal, and dignified. The British Shorthair is a sturdy, plush-coated companion.

Easy-going & Loyal 12-20 yrs

Sphynx

Hairless but full of love. Sphynx cats are extroverted, energetic, and crave human attention.

Extroverted & Energetic 8-14 yrs

Russian Blue

Elegant, reserved, and intelligent. Russian Blues have a shimmering silver-blue coat and emerald green eyes.

Reserved & Intelligent 15-20 yrs

Scottish Fold

Owl-like and sweet-tempered. Scottish Folds are known for their unique folded ears and loving personality.

Sweet & Adaptable 11-14 yrs

Birman

Gentle, quiet, and devoted. Birmans have silky coats, deep blue eyes, and white-gloved paws.

Gentle & Devoted 12-16 yrs

Abyssinian

Active, playful, and highly intelligent. One of the oldest known cat breeds with a ticked, wildcat look.

Active & Playful 9-15 yrs

Norwegian Forest Cat

Hardy, adventurous, and friendly. These large, fluffy cats were Viking companions and love the outdoors.

Friendly & Adventurous 12-16 yrs
