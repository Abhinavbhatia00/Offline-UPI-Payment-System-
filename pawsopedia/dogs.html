🐶 Dog Breeds

Explore our collection of popular dog breeds — from loyal Labradors to energetic Huskies.

Labrador Retriever

America's most popular breed. Friendly, outgoing, and high-spirited companions who have more than enough affection to go around.

Friendly & Outgoing 10-12 yrs

Golden Retriever

Intelligent, friendly, and devoted. Golden Retrievers are serious workers at hunting and as guide dogs, yet they love playtime.

Loyal & Intelligent 10-12 yrs

German Shepherd

Confident, courageous, and smart. The world's leading police, guard, and military dog — and a loving family pet.

Courageous & Confident 9-13 yrs

Beagle

Merry, friendly, and curious. Beagles are excellent family dogs, known for their nose and their happy-go-lucky temperament.

Curious & Merry 10-15 yrs

Siberian Husky

Loyal, mischievous, and outgoing. Built for endurance and speed, Huskies are strikingly beautiful arctic working dogs.

Energetic & Loyal 12-14 yrs

Poodle

Exceptionally smart and active. Poodles come in three sizes and are among the most trainable dogs in the world.

Intelligent & Active 12-15 yrs

Rottweiler

Loyal, loving, and confident guardian. Rottweilers are powerful working dogs with a gentle heart for their family.

Loyal & Confident 8-10 yrs

Doberman Pinscher

Fearless, loyal, and alert. Dobermans are among the world's finest protection dogs while being loving companions at home.

Fearless & Alert 10-12 yrs

Shih Tzu

Affectionate, happy, and outgoing. Bred to be a royal lap dog, the Shih Tzu is charming and devoted.

Affectionate & Happy 10-16 yrs

Border Collie

Remarkably bright workaholic. Border Collies are the Einstein of the dog world, excelling in agility and herding.

Smart & Energetic 12-15 yrs

Dalmatian

Dignified, smart, and outgoing. Famous for their spotted coat, Dalmatians are athletic and love to run.

Outgoing & Athletic 11-13 yrs

Pembroke Welsh Corgi

Bold, playful, and affectionate. Corgis are surprisingly athletic and love being part of the family action.

Playful & Bold 12-13 yrs
